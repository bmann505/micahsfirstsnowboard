First of Many

It was a clear blue sky, the sun was shining and the snow was fresh. Some might call it a bluebird day. The place? Lakewood Colorado. January 2017 Micah experienced his first snowboarding excursion. Granted it was at the local park, but you have to start somewhere.

 

He loves the snow and anything athletic. It is even better if there is some danger involved so Snowboarding and Micah are a match made in heaven. He is a first generation Coloradian but will learn the love of snow and mountains from his New Mexican parents.
